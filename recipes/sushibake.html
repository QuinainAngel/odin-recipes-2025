Easy sushi bake recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Sesame Oil
  • Imitation Crab
  • Mayo
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Cook rice according to instructions
  2. Add suagr, salt, and rice vinegar to rice once cooked
  3. Mix imitation crab and mayo
  4. Layer rice, then crab on top, then top with green onion
  5. Bake in oven
